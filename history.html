Historical Background of Pakistan

The history of Pakistan goes back thousands of years. The Indus Valley Civilization was one of the world’s earliest civilizations, with advanced cities such as Mohenjo-daro and Harappa.

Many empires ruled this region, including the Mughals. They introduced beautiful architecture, gardens, and cultural traditions that still influence Pakistan today.

Pakistan became an independent country on August 14, 1947, under the leadership of Muhammad Ali Jinnah. This was a major event in South Asian history.
