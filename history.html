Mohenjo-daro

Mohenjo-daro is one of the most important historical places in Pakistan. It is located in Sindh and was part of the Indus Valley Civilization. This ancient city shows advanced planning, drainage systems, and well-built houses.

Badshahi Mosque

The Badshahi Mosque is located in Lahore. It was built during the Mughal period and is one of the largest mosques in the world. The mosque is famous for its beautiful architecture and historical importance.

Rohtas Fort

Rohtas Fort is a large historical fort near Jhelum. It was built as a defensive structure and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The fort shows strong military architecture from the past.

Taxila

Taxila is an ancient archaeological site. It was once an important center of learning and Buddhist civilization. Today, it helps us understand the early history of the region.

Importance of Historical Places

  • They show Pakistan’s ancient civilizations
  • They reflect Islamic and Mughal architecture
  • They help preserve national heritage
  • They attract tourists from around the world
