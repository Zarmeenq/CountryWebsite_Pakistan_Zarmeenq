Traditions of Pakistan

Homepage Tradition History Food

Family plays a very important role in Pakistani society. People live in close-knit families and show great respect to elders. Important decisions are often made together, and family gatherings are common.

Traditional clothing includes the shalwar kameez, which is worn by both men and women. Women wear colorful dresses with dupattas, while men often wear waistcoats or turbans.

Festivals such as Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha are celebrated with prayers, special food, and family gatherings. Weddings include traditions like mehndi, music, and dancing.
