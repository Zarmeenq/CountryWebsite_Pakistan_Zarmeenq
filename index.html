About Pakistan:

Pakistan is a country in South Asia, officially called the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. Its capital city is Islamabad, and the largest city is Karachi. Pakistan was established in 1947 and shares borders with India, Afghanistan, Iran, and China. The national language is Urdu, while English is widely used for official purposes. Pakistan has a population of over 240 million people, making it one of the most populous countries in the world. The country’s geography includes mountains, deserts, plains, and a coastline along the Arabian Sea.

Background Information About Pakistan:

Pakistan is a country located in South Asia. It shares borders with India, China, Afghanistan, and Iran, and has a coastline along the Arabian Sea. Pakistan is known for its rich culture, deep history, and beautiful landscapes—from tall mountains like the K2 to wide deserts and green valleys.

It was founded in 1947 as a homeland for Muslims in the Indian subcontinent. Today, Pakistan is a diverse country with many languages, traditions, and lifestyles. The people of Pakistan are known for their hospitality, strong family values, and cultural pride.

Pakistan is made up of four main provinces: Punjab, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, and Balochistan. Each region has its own customs, clothing, food, and celebrations, which together make Pakistan a colorful and vibrant country.
